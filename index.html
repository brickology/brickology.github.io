Thinking about a LEGO event?

Kids

Celebrate your child’s birthday with a LEGO party! Your kid will be built into a personalized LEGO tower, and 12-24 friends can play with more than 100 pounds of bricks. Themed building (like Star Wars or castles) is also available. Younger children can enjoy a DUPLO party, with a 99' train and various themed projects.

Schools

The mission of after-school LEGO class is to provide a safe and nurturing environment for holistic enrichment through guided play and modeling of behavior. Your child will learn my most important building skills of overlapping and symmetry while enjoying original activities including: arrays, art, braille, bridges and arches, buoyancy, catapults, dollars and cents, flags of the world, fractions, games, gears, hinges, letters and numbers, patterns, polygons, magnetism, mazes, mosaics, pentominoes, pneumatics, reflection, shapes, tessellations, transparent illumination, 2D vs. 3D, wheels, axles and rotation.

Organizations

Beth has experience working with scouts, libraries, schools, daycares, park districts, dorms, corporations, conventions, and more. Whether you want a free build, a customized LEGO tower, or themed building/learning, there’s an activity for you. These events can host up to 100 people, depending on the organization.
